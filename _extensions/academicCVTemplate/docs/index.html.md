# academicCVTemplate

Welcome to the official documentation for `academicCVTemplate`, a Quarto extension designed to help you create beautiful, modern, and professional academic CVs and cover letters with ease. This extension leverages the power of the Typst typesetting system to produce high-quality, print-ready PDF documents.

## Why Use This Template?

*   **High-Quality Typesetting:** Built on Typst, a modern and powerful typesetting system, ensuring your documents look clean, professional, and are perfectly formatted.
*   **Data-Driven Content:** Seamlessly integrate your CV data from Google Sheets, allowing you to keep your information up-to-date in one central location.
*   **Highly Customizable:** Easily change colors, fonts, and layout options through a simple YAML configuration.
*   **All-in-One:** Generate both your academic CV and a matching cover letter from a single Quarto document.
